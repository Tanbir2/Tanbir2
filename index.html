My Journey

My journey in electrical engineering began with a strong academic foundation during my undergraduate studies in Electrical Engineering, where I developed core knowledge in power systems, circuit analysis, and electrical machines. During this time, I became particularly interested in understanding how modern power systems can be made more reliable, efficient, and intelligent.

As part of my undergraduate studies, I worked on a research-focused thesis on transmission line fault classification using machine learning techniques. In this project, I applied MLPNN and ensemble learning algorithms to improve fault detection accuracy. This experience introduced me to the integration of data-driven methods with traditional power engineering and played a key role in shaping my technical interests.

To further advance my expertise, I am currently pursuing a Master of Engineering (MEng) in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Calgary, specializing in power systems and sustainable energy. Through this program, I have developed a deeper understanding of power system analysis, industrial power systems, renewable energy integration, and machine learning applications in engineering.

Alongside my academic studies, I have focused on building practical, industry-relevant skills using tools such as ETAP, MATLAB/Simulink, Python, and PSS®E. I have applied these tools in projects involving load flow analysis, fault studies, and data-driven power system modeling.

Currently, I am working on an automated 8760-hour power system reliability and curtailment analysis framework using Python and PSS®E. This project focuses on evaluating system performance under high renewable penetration and contingency scenarios, reflecting real-world challenges in modern power systems.

My goal is to build a career in power systems, particularly in transmission, protection, and system operations, while contributing to the development of reliable and sustainable energy infrastructure. I am especially interested in applying intelligent technologies such as machine learning and automation to enhance system performance and support the transition toward cleaner energy systems.
